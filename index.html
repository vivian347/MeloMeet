Creating a Room

This is a view in our website that allows hosts to create a personalized room. On clicking the "Create Room" button, a unique code is generated for easy sharing with friends, thus, allowing them to easily join the room.

Additionally, the host is provided with play/pause and skip media functionality which are all backed by essential database models.

This is Our Story

Our journey began with a desire to bridge the gap between friends separated by distance. We envisioned a world where music could be the thread that unites us all, regardless of where we are in the world. We knew that sharing music was one of the best ways to connect with others, and we wanted to make it as easy as possible.

This led us to create a platform that would allow friends to share music seamlessly, no matter how far apart they were and we a re committed to continuing our journey to create a world where music is the language that unites us all.
